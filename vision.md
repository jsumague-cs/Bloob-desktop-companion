# Vision

## Mission

Bloob exists to make productivity feel warm, encouraging, and human.

Many productivity applications focus on deadlines, numbers, and pressure. Bloob takes a different approach by becoming a friendly desktop companion that encourages users through kindness rather than guilt.

## Vision

To create an AI companion that helps people build healthy habits while making them feel supported instead of judged.

## Problem Statement

People often procrastinate because tasks feel overwhelming. Existing productivity tools can be cold, complicated, or stressful.

Bloob aims to reduce this friction by combining intelligent reminders, conversational AI, and an engaging companion into one application.

## Core Values

- Kindness over pressure
- Progress over perfection
- Consistency over intensity
- Privacy first
- User control
